Live The Scene

SPARC’s in-house Clear Audio technology automatically adjusts the sound setting so as to provide you with a rich listening experience. It optimizes various audio formats to deliver a clear high-quality sound. With rear speakers, external subwoofer work and a 2.0 channel soundbar that delivers authentic theater surround sound.

Audio In

SPARC VIBE P60 Sound Bar System is equipped with a portable input port. It allows you to enjoy latest music along with corresponding digital content from a portable device. You can connect your MP3 players, laptops, and personal multimedia player to the sound entertainment system.

Sound technology transforms high-quality stereo signals into multi-channel surround sounds for a more immersive aural experience.

Connection On-The-Go

This sound bar is compatible with a variety of devices such as, TVs, gaming consoles, BD/DVD players and MP3 players. Stream all the music you love through your soundbar, courtesy of Bluetooth.
