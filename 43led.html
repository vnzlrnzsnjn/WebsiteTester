Full HD Experience

It’s time to move to FULL HD. With High Definition of 1920 x 1080 pixels, you can get more than 4 times of images from the conventional Standard Definition TVs that will give you crystal clear images. Sparc TV exhibits a naturalistic colors and its intensity of realism will give you full enjoyment when you watch movies and shows.

3D Comb Filter

This HDTV technology has the comb filter that separates the luminance signal from the chrominance signal three horizontal scan lines of video at a time. This filter processes incoming images, making sure that what you see is color-accurate and reduced video noise resulting to a clearer and sharper video picture

Brillance in Colors

Ultra HD gives you more than 8 million pixels while a Full HD has an estimated 2 million pixels- the result is picture clarity so real you might reach out to feel and touch it. Colours pop and motion is seamless and natural. Each pixel is resilient for stunning brightness and detail.

Clear Motion Rate

Get ultra-smooth motion from SPARC’s interpolation feature. Different levels of motion enhancement can be selected to fit your comfort level. You can enjoy fast moving scenes bringing clarity right in front of you.
